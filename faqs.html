How often should I water my plants?

It depends on the plant and season. A general rule is to water when the top inch of soil feels dry. Overwatering is more dangerous than underwatering!

How do I know if my plant is getting too much or too little light?

Yellowing leaves may indicate too much light, while pale or leggy growth could mean too little. Observe and adjust placement accordingly.
